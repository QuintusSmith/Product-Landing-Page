Redragon Toccata

Professional Gaming Speakers

The Redragon 11W Toccata RGB 2.1 PC Gaming Speaker Set offers great sound with deep bass and colourful RGB backlighting effects. Its unique futuristic design is great for the gamer who wants to make a statement with both the sound and in terms of looks. It features 2 x 3W speakers and a 5W subwoofer for bass. It comes with a volume and bass control knob, a power switch as well as a switch for the LED lighting. The speakers are powered via USB which can be plugged directly into the subwoofer. The subwoofer itself is great for adding that additional bass to your gaming, movies or music.
